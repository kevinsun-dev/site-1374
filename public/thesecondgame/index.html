How to Play
This is the second of six games, a staircase maze. Find your way through the maze to reach the exit at the end. However, to open a door, you must first solve a puzzle. All puzzles are provided to you from the start, but only the puzzle corresponding to the room number can open it's doors.

Elimination
If a player cannot leave the maze before November 4th, at 11:59 PM, they will be eliminated from the games.

Good luck, and have fun! :D
