Welcome to the games, Player. We’re glad to have you here.

How to Play
This is the first of six games, a simple game of Bingo. Fill in Bingo squares until a row, column, or diagonal is complete. However, to fill in a square, you must first solve a puzzle. The answer to each puzzle will be a single word unless specified otherwise.

Unlocking Puzzles
We have scattered around QR codes to unlock puzzles across the campus. When scanned, the puzzle is unlocked for everyone, and a unique hint is revealed only to the player who unlocked it. Please take down the QR after you’ve scanned it. We’ve included a list of locations to where the unlock codes can be found.

Elimination
If a player cannot complete a Bingo before October 27th, at 11:59 PM, they will be eliminated from the games.

Good luck, and have fun! :D
